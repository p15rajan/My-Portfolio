WebGIS — Sathyamangalam Tiger Reserve: Animal & Plant Inventory

Developed a full-stack interactive WebGIS application for the Sathyamangalam Tiger Reserve (STR), Tamil Nadu — one of India's critical tiger corridors spanning ~1,411 km² across the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve. The platform enables forest rangers, ecologists, and researchers to digitally record, query, and visualize geo-tagged wildlife sightings and flora distribution across reserve zones in real time.

Animal Inventory

Geo-tagged sighting records for tigers, leopards, elephants, gaur, sloth bears, and 200+ bird species with GPS coordinates, date, zone, and observer notes.

Plant Inventory

Spatial distribution of endemic and endangered flora — teak, rosewood, sandalwood, medicinal plants — mapped with density and conservation status attributes.

Map Layers

Reserve boundary, buffer zones, beat/range divisions, water bodies, forest type (LULC), roads, patrol routes — served as WMS/WFS via GeoServer.

Query & Filter

Attribute & spatial queries via PostGIS — filter by species, date range, zone, or draw a bounding box on the Leaflet map to fetch records from the database.
